WHO WE ARE

At Black Horse, we offer a realxed and upbeat environment that invites our guests to enjoy the expertly made coffee beverages, teas, hot chocolate, Italian sodas, paninis, and our famous
baked goods.
Whether a cappuccino, mocha, or coffee, you can rest assured that it will be the best drink of pastry money can buy.

WHY BLACK HORSE

We pride ourselves in using only certified Fair Trade organic coffee beans, delivered fresh each week and equipping our baristas with top-of-the line espresso machines, allowing them to craft top-quality coffee beverages.
Thanks for stopping by and we are so excited to serve you.
